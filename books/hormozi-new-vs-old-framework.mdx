Ad Framework #2: New vs. Old (Complete Script Template)

Why This Ad Framework Rocks

This is a classic ADucational framework. It works especially well with advanced or complex products and services. It positions you as an authority while driving both brand recognition and sales. If you're brand new, this format helps you get judged by the quality of information rather than your brand. If you're established, it reinforces authority even more.

Visual Hook
• Film in an educational setting (whiteboard, flip chart, etc.).
• Whiteboard = educational = value.
• Works better with more complex products.

Ad Copy

Hook (Old vs. New Intro)
"[CALL OUT] Hey [AVATAR], let me show you how we took [old metric, e.g. $1,500/mo clients] and turned them into [new metric, e.g. $15,000/mo clients] by switching from [old model] to [new mechanism]."

Meat/Offer
1. OLD WAY (Pain)
"Here's why most people get stuck: with the retainer model, you charge a flat fee and clients pay X dollars per month. But the value you provide changes over time. The second they feel the value dip below what they're paying, they cancel. That's the flaw of the old way."

2. PROBLEM
"It's unpredictable, churn is high, and you can't scale because every client is capped at a flat rate."

3. NEW WAY (Opportunity)
"But what if you flipped the model? Instead of charging a flat retainer, you charge per outcome — something measurable and valuable every time. That way, the value always exists. The more outcomes you deliver, the more you earn."

4. DREAM OUTCOME
"Now you're no longer capped. The client actually wants you to scale, because the more you deliver, the more money they make."

5. PROOF (Case study or Hypothetical)
"For example, instead of $1,500 per month flat, imagine charging $100 per show. On day 30, instead of asking for double the retainer, you just double the ad spend — and clients say yes, because ROI is proven."

6. RESULTS (Authority)
"We've generated [single proof stat, e.g. $98.5M in one niche] using this switch. Just one number is enough to prove this works."

CTA (Call to Action)
1. Preview the Process
"If you want to see exactly how this model works, click below to apply. We'll confirm if you qualify and then map out your numbers on a 15-minute call."

2. Risk Reversal (if possible)
"If you don't make money, I don't get paid."

3. Close with Hook Restated
"I'm looking for [#] [AVATAR] doing roughly [current metric] who want [10x outcome] by swapping their [old model] for a [new mechanism]. We've already proven it with [single proof stat], and if you don't profit, I don't get paid. Click to apply — my team will verify fit and show you the math."

How To Apply This Framework
1. Big Claim with Details
Ex: "We turned $1,500/month clients into $15,000/month clients."
Big increase + same context = curiosity.

2. Tie Pain to Outdated Model
Ex: "Flat retainers cause cancellations, unpredictability, and no scalability."

3. Reveal the New Way + Metric
Ex: "We rewrote the rule: $100 per show instead of a retainer."

4. Add Case Study or Hypothetical
Ex: "On day 30, instead of asking for more retainer, you double ad spend and double the results."

5. Reinforce with Proof Line
Ex: "We've already generated $98.5M in one niche with this switch."

6. Risk-Reversed Offer
Ex: "If you don't profit, I don't get paid."

7. CTA
Ex: "Click Apply, qualify, and we'll map your numbers on a 15-minute call."
