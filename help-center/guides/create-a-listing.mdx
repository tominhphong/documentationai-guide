## Overview

Follow these steps to add a new property listing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Log in and click "Dashboard" on the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Add New Listing">
    Press the "Add New Listing" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in property info">
    Complete fields: address, price, details, and add photos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click "Publish" to make your listing live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
You can view or edit listings anytime from your dashboard.
</Callout>
