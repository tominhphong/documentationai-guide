title: Unable to Upload Photos
description: Troubleshoot photo upload issues in Realtor OS.
---

## Symptoms

- Photo upload fails or does not complete
- Error: "Upload failed" or "File too large"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check file size and format">
    Images should be JPG or PNG and less than 10MB.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and retry">
    Reload the page and try uploading again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Clear browser cache and cookies if issue persists.
  </Step>
</Steps>
